Cleanup Instructions

PWA Cleanup

Analysts should start by removing the PWA from their home screens, followed by clearing browser data:

  1. Long-press the PWA icon on your home screen and select the option to remove or uninstall it.
  2. Open your browser and navigate to the settings menu.
  3. Look for options to clear the cache, cookies, history, and browser settings, typically found under 'Privacy', 'History', or 'Storage' settings.

Browser Cleanup

For iOS (Safari and other browsers)

Follow these steps to manually clear browser data:

  1. Open 'Settings' on your iOS device and select 'Safari' or your preferred browser.
  2. Tap on 'Clear History and Website Data' under the 'Privacy & Security' section.
  3. For a more thorough cleanup, you may need to navigate to 'Advanced' settings to clear additional website data.

For Android Browsers

To clear all browser data, you can simply uninstall the browser:

  1. Go to 'Settings' and select 'Apps' or 'Application Manager'.
  2. Find the browser app, tap on it, and then select 'Uninstall'.
  3. Confirm the uninstallation to complete the cleanup.
